BBQ Kielbasa Sausage over Rice

A simple two-ingredient preparation over a bed of rice of your chosing. Easy to make for appetizers during tailgating season!

Ingredients

  • 1 kielbasa sausage
  • 1/3 cup Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ Sauce
  • rice
  • (optional) vegetables

Procedure

  1. Cut the sausage into 1/2 inch slices and then cut in half.
  2. Cook in a skillet pan and after the meat is cooked thoroughly, add the BBQ sauce and cook for another 4-6 minutes, stirring the BBQ sauce in with the meat.
  3. Cook the rice per directions on the box.
  4. Pour the kielbasa on top of the rice when on serving plates.
