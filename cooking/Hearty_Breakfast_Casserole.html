Hearty Breakfast Casserole

When you're tired of cereal and want a hearty, warm breakfast like mom makes, this is an easy recipe that has all the breakfast classics in one dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. mild or hot ground pork sausage
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup Bisquick
  • (Optional) 1 package frozen hash browns

Procedure

  1. Cook sausage in a large skillet. Cook until no longer pink. Drain.
  2. Spray baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
  3. Mix together hash browns, sausage, and cheese. Pour into a sightly greased 13- x 9-inch baking dish.
  4. Mix eggs, milk, Bisquick, and pepper.
  5. Pour egg mixture over the sausage and cheese mixture.
  6. Bake at 400Â° for 35 to 40 minutes.
  7. (Optional) Sprinkle shredded cheese after finished cooking and put dish back in until cheese is melted.
  8. (Optional) Hashbrowns can be cooked and added into the sausage and cheese mixture.

Results

Every bite of this was so full of flavor. It made for an easy breakfast on the go since I could reheat a slice each morning in the microwave.
